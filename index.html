About Me

Hi, I'm Alec Roland — a finance student at BYU's Marriott School of Business, husband, and aspiring finance professional. I grew up in Spokane, WA and now live in Provo, UT with my wife. I hold a 3.91 GPA and a full-ride academic scholarship, and I'm currently applying to BYU's Finance program.

I'm driven by a passion for financial markets, problem-solving, and building things that matter. I currently intern at Write Grader, an AI grading startup, where I prospect universities and deliver product demos. Previously I worked as a Financial Advisor Assistant at PacNorth Retirement in Spokane, helping clients through a broker-dealer transition.

Experience & Skills

My work experience spans financial modeling, wealth management, and AI technology sales. In February 2026 I competed in the Cougar Tech AI Assist Case Competition, building an 18-assumption financial model and presenting a 12-slide pricing strategy pitch. I'm also a certified phlebotomist and an active member of BYU's Finance Society.

International Experience

From 2023 to 2024 I lived in Bangkok, Thailand for two years as a volunteer representative. I became fluent in Thai, organized a 50-person flood relief effort in northern Thailand, and built a language tutoring program for incoming volunteers. It was a defining chapter that shaped how I think about leadership, adaptability, and service. Read more on my Adventures page.

Personal

Outside of school and work I enjoy spending time with my wife, following financial news, and staying active. I speak Thai fluently and conversational Lao, and I love any excuse to use them. Feel free to reach out — I'm always happy to connect.
